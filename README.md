**Key Market Drivers**
**Electrification and ADAS Integration**

Electric vehicles (EVs) and ADAS are redefining glazing requirements, requiring advanced laminated, tempered, and smart glass with integrated sensors and heads‑up capabilities. Manufacturers investing in these technologies are gaining strategic supplier advantage.

**Safety & Regulation**

Stringent safety regulations across North America, Europe, and Asia Pacific are mandating higher standards for laminated windshields and tempered glass, expanding market demand and elevating aftermarket services.

**Consumer Preferences**

Demand for privacy, solar control, heating elements, and aesthetic customization is increasing, particularly in luxury and EV segments—boosting the value of premium glass solutions.

**Supply Chain Volatility**
